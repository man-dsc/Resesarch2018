This web-based tool is a Python implementaton of MAPFEAT, a method that introduces software features to emergency apps by automating the mining of needs from general purpose tweets and their translation into real app features. The tool has been evaluated by a case study of the 2016 wildfire in Fort McMurray, Canada, in which it identified a significant amount of new and valuable functionality.
